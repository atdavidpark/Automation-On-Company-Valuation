Income Statement:
	Total Sales = all components of sales
	COGS excl. D&A = COGS incl. D&A - Depreciation & Amortization Expense
	SG&A Expense (Selling, General, and Administrative Expenses) = ratio by Total Sales
	EBIT and Operating Income are the same. EBIT (Earnings before Interest and Taxes) = Gross Income - D&A Expense - SG&A Expense - Other Operating Expense
	Net Unusual Expense = ratio by EBIT
	Pretax Income = EBIT + Net Nonoperating Income - Interest Expense - Net Unusual Expense
	Income Taxes = ratio by Pretax Income
	Net Income = Pretax Income - Income Taxes
	EPS (diluted) = Net Income / Diluted Shares Outstanding
	EBITDA (Earnings before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, and Amortization) = EBIT + D&A Expense
